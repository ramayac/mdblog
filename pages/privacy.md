This privacy policy applies to the Android applications (the "Apps") developed by Rodrigo Amaya. It governs your use of the Apps and explains how we handle any information you provide or that is collected through the Apps.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Our primary goal in collecting information is to provide and improve our Apps and services. 

- **Personal Data:** We do not collect or request any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number) unless you explicitly contact us for support or feedback.
- **Non-Personal / Usage Data:** Our Apps do not automatically collect or transmit any analytical data, device identifiers, or usage statistics to external servers, except as described in the third-party services section below.

## 2. Third-Party Services

Depending on the specific App, we may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
- **Google Play Services:** Used to facilitate app distribution, updates, and platform features. Google Play Services has its own privacy policy which you can review [here](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Payment / Donation links:** If you use external links within our Apps (such as Ko-fi, PayPal, or GitHub Sponsors) to support our work, your transactions are handled entirely by those third parties under their respective privacy policies.

## 3. Data Security

We value your trust. Since we do not collect or store your personal data on any servers of our own, there is no personal information database at risk. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

These Apps do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our records.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

I am excited to introduce my first Android application! It's a lightweight utility designed to help users organize their daily routines and track goals seamlessly.

## Key Features

- **Minimalist Interface:** Clean, distraction-free layout to keep you focused.
- **Offline First:** All data is stored locally on your device for maximum privacy.
- **Fast Performance:** Written in native Kotlin with Jetpack Compose.
